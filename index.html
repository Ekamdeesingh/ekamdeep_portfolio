About Ekamdeep

A physiotherapy student learning to combine science, movement and empathy to help people move with confidence again.

I am currently in my 1st year of Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) at Lovely Professional University. My classes and labs revolve around understanding the human body, muscle function, and safe ways to improve movement.

I enjoy seeing how small changes in posture, strength and daily routines can reduce pain and improve quality of life. With each semester, I want to become better at listening to people and guiding them through a calm, structured recovery process.
